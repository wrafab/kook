Final Project



For my final project, I created a 3D illusion on a rotating plate using 3D printing, coreldraw, laser cutting, and arduino uno.

First, I designed and printed out the 3D model using Autodesk Fusion 360.

3D Illusion



I sketched my name on xz plane and RESERVE'17 on a new plane I created in 45 degrees angle,

and I extruded my name and made a hole with RESERVE'17.

This is my first 3D model I printed out.



When I printed, the support was necessary in order to create the holes with letters.

I had to get rid of all the supports using different tools.

This was my biggest challenge because for my first model, the holes was so tiny that it was very hard to reach in.







After the long time of struggle, I decided to print a better model because it was impossible to get all the supports out from my first model.

I designed a model with larger texts used as the holes, and used the simple font as possible. And here's my second model printed.






Another round of removing the supports.



The result was much better.
