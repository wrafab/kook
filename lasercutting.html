Laser Cutting 360




This week's task is to work with laser cutting!


For practice, I designed a cardboard box using MakerCase.

I measured the length, width, and the thickness of the cardboard first. Then, I adjusted the box width, height, and depth as I desire. I chose finger edge joints.



Using Coreldraw, I added some letters to raster.



After defining which lines to vector or raster, I was ready to cut my box. This is the box I created using laser cutting!








Making a stamp using laser cutting



Using Corel Draw, I designed my stamp and using the lasercutting machine, Epilog Laser Fusion M2, I cut out my stamp.
This is the timelapse video of lasercutting my stamp on rubber.





After cutting the rubber, I glued the rubber onto a piece of wood, and this is what my stamp looks like.







Making a puzzle using laser cutting




Using Corel Draw, I drew random curves to make a puzzle. I tried to make many grooves as possible to get the pieces stay together. It was interesting to see how accurately the puzzle got cut.
