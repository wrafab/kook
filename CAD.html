3D Designing using Autodesk Fusion 360

The task was to design an enclosure for an Arduino Uno.

First thing to do was to create a sketch. I created a sketch for each of the top cover part and the bottom part. I first thought about the connecting part of the top and bottom before I started sketching. I wanted some holes on the bottom and also a groove on both sides of the case to make it easier to open the case. I wanted everything to stay in a perfect dimension, so I added a lot of sketch dimensions.
