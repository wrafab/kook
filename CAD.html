3D Designing using Autodesk Fusion 360

The task was to design an enclosure for an Arduino Uno.

First thing to do was to imagine how my UNO case would look like. I thought about how the connecting part of the top and bottom would be before I started sketching. To help with imagination, I sketched the model with dimensions I want on a piece of paper



Now, with my sketch on the paper, I actually started sketching on Autodesk Fusion 360. I created a sketch for each of the top cover part and the bottom part. I have created some holes on the bottom and also some grooves on both sides of the case to make it easier to open the case.

This is my final bottom plane sketch on just the bottom plane. I wanted everything to stay in a perfect dimension, so I added a lot of sketch dimensions.
