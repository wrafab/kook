Printing Arduino Uno Case



Finally, I printed out the Arduino Uno Case I designed a few weeks ago.



I didn't realize until I printed out that when I assemble the top and the bottom pieces together, they would be mirroring each other. So the holes ended up not matching as shown below.




I also found that my name on the top part of the case wasn't clear. If I had made the resolution to be higher, that might have helped.






3D printing the stamp case




I decided to create a stamp box for the stamp I made. I designed a box, using fusion360.
I wanted the height of the lower part to be shorter than the height of the stamp to make it easier to take the stamp out.
I also created grooves on the sides of the top part to make it easier to open.
Then, I added the design of the stamp on top of the case to show which stamp is in it.


This is what the case looks like after printing.




I'm so happy that it came out exactly as I wanted to be.
