Vinyl Cutting and Printing using Roland GS-24 Vinyl Cutter



Vinyl Sticker



Using Coreldraw, I edited the photo of my dog, cropping out its face and adding a sketch filter.
I then added a contour to the picture to give a black padding.
Lastly, I selected the border of the picture and selected it as a pink line to indicate which lines should be cut.


This is a video of the stickers getting printed.




After printing the picture, the cutter cuts the borders of the stickers.




This is the final product.







Contour Stickers



This time instead of printing, using just cutting, I made contour stickers.
I used Coreldraw to make the sticker designs.
To remove all backgrounds, I converted pictures into bitmap and traced the outline of the pictures

I wanted my stickers to be black, so I took the roll of black sticker paper and set it in the cutter.



This is what looks like after the stickers were cut.



Then, I took all unnecessary part, leaving just stickers.



I put the transfer film over the stickers and cut them into single pieces



I put sunglasses sticker on the apple on my mac.



And a dog on my calculator!
